Hi! I am Manasa Hari.

I am a Senior Software Engineer III at Adobe, and one of the core engineers behind the Adobe Experience Platform (AEP) Real-Time Customer Data “Collaboration” Platform — Adobe’s latest innovation in digital advertising.

I played a pivotal role as a key engineer in the core team for the AEP Real-Time CDP Collaboration General Availability (GA) launch, successfully released on February 20, 2025.

My work at Adobe includes:

  • Building innovative portal features that enhance decision-making within the AEP ecosystem
  • Developing tools that enable real-time, privacy-first collaboration across brands and partners
  • Designing scalable workflows that integrate diverse data sources seamlessly
  • Partnering closely with product managers, UX designers, architects, and data engineers to translate business requirements into technical solutions
  • Participating in architecture and design reviews to ensure alignment with broader platform strategy
  • Writing, testing, and maintaining high-quality, reusable, production-grade code
  • Optimizing workflows for performance, reliability, and scale
  • Analyzing user feedback to continuously improve product capabilities

Previously, I served as a Senior Customer Solutions Engineer at Selector AI.

Across more than a decade of experience working in the United States, Malaysia, and India, I have built scalable, data-driven systems and collaborated with diverse global teams. This journey has shaped my ability to deeply understand customer needs and design solutions that are technically sound, resilient, and business-aligned.

Prior to Adobe and Selector AI, I worked at prominent companies including Splunk, RingCentral, Taulia, and Celcom Malaysia, where I contributed to distributed systems, API architectures, and large-scale data platforms.

I earned my Master’s degree in Software Engineering from San Jose State University (SJSU) and completed my undergraduate degree in Electronics & Computer Engineering (ECM) from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK) in 2012.

I am passionate about building scalable platforms, architecting resilient systems, and shaping the future of privacy-first digital collaboration.
