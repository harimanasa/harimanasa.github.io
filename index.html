Hi! I'm Manasa Hari

As a Senior Customer Solutions Engineer at Selector AI, I collaborate closely with clients to gain insights into their data and analytics requirements and implement software solutions that facilitate seamless interaction with Selector's platform for operational intelligence.

I have garnered a decade of valuable experience through employment at various companies across multiple countries (United States, Malaysia, India). This journey has equipped me with the ability to collaborate seamlessly with diverse teams and comprehend distinct customer needs, allowing me to empathize with and incorporate those considerations into my software engineering.

Prior to my tenure at Selector AI, I held positions at several prominent companies, including Splunk, RingCentral, Taulia, and Celcom, Malaysia.

I earned a Master's degree from San Jose State University (SJSU) in Software Engineering. I completed my undergraduate from Department of Electronics & Computer Engineering (ECM) from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada (JNTUK) in 2012.
